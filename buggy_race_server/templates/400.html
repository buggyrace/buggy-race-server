Bad Request

This problem is nearly always because your request didn't have the correct CSRF token.
The racing server puts a token into every form, and when you click a button it checks that the token matches what it expected.
These tokens timeout, and that's probably what happened here.

Go back and refresh the page you came from
and then try to click on the button again.
That will probably solve the problem.

What are CSRF tokens? Why does this happen?

These Cross-site Request Forgery tokens prevent someone hijacking your login by pretending to be a request you sent.
